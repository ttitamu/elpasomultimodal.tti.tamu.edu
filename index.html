Visualizing the El Paso Regional Multimodal Survey

The El Paso Regional Multimodal Survey was developed by the Texas A&M Transportation Institute for the El Paso Metropolitan Planning Organization (MPO) as part of its multimodal plan. The survey captured a representative sample of 1,505 residents from the El Paso MPO planning area and covered a range of topics including transportation habits, attitudes toward alternative transportation modes, as well as personal health and well-being.
